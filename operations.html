Cultural and Cross-Cultural Missions

The focus of the mission arm of this ministry are:

  • We are engaged in outreaches to campuses and churches for the purpose of revival and mobilization for missions.
  • Link intending missionaries to the appropriate mission schools and theological institutions where they can be trained for mission work.
  • To hold missionaries banquet, a forum where missionaries will gather for refreshment, spirit, soul and body.
  • We foster networking among missionaries.Â 
  • The mission arm coordinates the vision and mission school.
  • The mission arm coordinates our gospel invasion crusade
  • The mission arm shall also coordinate the 6 hour monthly retreat for church workers and ministers.
  • This arm is also involved in church planting before handing over to a capable hand or a bible believing ministry.
  • Short and long term health care and legal aid services as a means of evangelism both to rural and urban areas.

Garrison Takers Summit

It is yearly gathering of the remaining reserved seven thousand (7000) whose knees have not bowed to baal (worldliness) nor eaten on the table of corruption but who are truthfully and quietly laboring, eagerly but patiently waiting for the second coming of Jesus. It is a forum to recruit men for training in the school of vision and mission. It is also a gathering to foster unity in the body of Christ. It is forum for bridging the gap between the older generations who have witnessed revival in the sixties and in the seventies and the younger generations who are just coming into it

School of Vision and Mission[SOVAM]

The school was established mainly to make the youths understand how plenteous the harvest is, refocus them from frivolity, fine up their zeal for kingdom exploits and train them to discover their God ordained calling.
In summary, the school helps young people discover their calling and pilot them into it.

 

Widows Ministry

James 1 vs 27. (Pure religion andundefiled before God and the Father is this, To visit the fatherless and widows in their affliction, and to keep himself unspotted from the world). Our labour among the widows has some specifics as God has directed us and they are highlighted below:
1. To encourage, comfort and support the widows, spirit, soul and body
2. To educate and provide the necessary information for younger widows on how to re-marry through teachings, prayers and counseling.
3. To help the older widows realize their place of relevance in ministry
4. To establish vocational trainingcenters where widows shall be taught requisite skills to help them earn a living.
5. To organize widows conference periodically
