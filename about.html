About Ark of God World Misssion

It is an evangelistic mission agency committed to birthing revival both in the rural and urban centres through wholistic approach to mission work. We are a team of people that are passionately engaged in refocusing the church for missions and helping the youths to discover and find a place of relevance in ministry.
We achieve this through mission mobilization, leadership training and youth empowerment. Our mission work also includes family revival and giving hope and comfort to widows and orphans.
We trust God to greatly enrich your heart through this forum as you surf through this knowledge base and also open your eyes to your part in the end-time revival labour so that together we can fulfill the great commission and enhance the coming of the king (Jesus). God bless you!

Our Vission

To reach and establish the unreached In Christ with the gospel and teach the religious to practice Christ centered faith until men become capable and available for the end-time harvest.

 

Our Mission

To bring comfort to the helpless, hopeless and the comfortless through aggressive pursuit of both urban and rural outreaches, mission mobilization, leadership training, child development and family revival.
